AboutUs


Deli Kitchen is a small business founded by Zephyr Borseth. It is the outskirts of the limpopo region. When he was studying in collage he found out that a lot students could not cook,neven basic food. This inspired him to start cooking classes in the dorm of his collage and a lot of students joined his classes so much that he made enough money to open his cooking school in polokwane, after he graduated thus the Deli Kitchen was found

His mission is to give the youth an opportunity to learn cooking so that they may be able to cook for themselves without relying on street food. the vision would like to see in the youth is the youth can have majestic skills for cooking, not just for survival but also to preserve the delicasies of food. Over the years he has hired chefs to help him run the school and help those who want to specilase in whatever category they would like to learn. each chef is a proffetional chef with qualifications.
